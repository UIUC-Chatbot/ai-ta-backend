New Features 

Web Crawling

Our web crawling + scraping is getting seriously powerful.

  • It’s crazy fast (upwards of 2GB/s of crawling speed!)
  • It’s doing full Javascript rendering, no content is missed or lost. 
  • We grab full PDF files while crawling. 
  • Total re-write of our crawler to find every possible page on a website. It’s based on the incredible open-source crawlee.dev.

Read the docs

Access it on your Materials page: uiuc.chat/YOUR_PROJECT/materials

 

Here’s what to look for. Just enter any URL to crawl that site. It runs in the background. Feel free to start multiple crawls from different parts on the same website to get full coverage.

Document Groups

Document Groups help you find what you need faster.
You can:

  • Organize documents into clear, manageable categories
  • Enable and disable Document Groups to control what your chatbot users see.
  • Chat with subsets of your data using filters (coming soon to the /chat page)

Try it out and start navigating huge projects with ease and control

More features coming in hot

  1. Custom tool use by the LLMs. We provide a drag-n-drop “worfklow builder” interface (thanks n8n.io) where you can build complex workflows all with a GUI. Way easier than OpenAI’s version of tools.
  2. Getting data into your chat: imagine a real-time API connection to your email, Jira tickets or Notion. The LLM can query these on an as-needed basis using LLM function calling. 
  3. Taking action from chat: Now that you’ve analyzed some data and come to a conclusion, you can authorize the LLM to send an email with a summary of your findings, or update a Jira ticket. 
  4. LLM Guided Retrieval. Finding your most relevant documents in a sea of millions is the most important. We just submitted a grant to Amazon’s Illinois Center to fund developing refining this idea. But we’ve started building it already. See the diagram below.
